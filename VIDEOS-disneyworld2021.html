Disney World 2021

Recorded 23-31 August, 2021

My vacation to Disney World in August 2021!

Photos

23 August

On the plane preparing to disembark; excited, but tired because I got literally no sleep the night before due to allergies!

Later that evening we went to look at the new Riviera Resort; we were staying at Pop Century!

25 August

We visited Animal Kingdom and went on Kilimanjaro Safaris. I took a few animal photos.

Oh yeah then two fucking rhinos ran into the road a foot in front of our moving truck and we almost died :D

Later that day we went to rest at the Animal Kingdom Lodge. My hair getting like this is one of the many reasons why I don't live in Florida.

26 August

We ate at Beaches and Cream this day, our first time since their new redesign. I'm honestly not the biggest fan of the redesign; it loses a lot of the original theming. I had the footlong hot dog though, which I loved!

28 August

I took a photo out of our hotel room's peephole this day!

Later at Disney Springs, my grandparents bought me a hat to solve my hair issue and my grandfather bought me a pair of Ray Bans as a gift!

30 August

I got a better photo of an Okapi on this day; later we ate at Rainforest Cafe and I took a low-light photo of the Corona logo.

31 August

I took this photo of myself with the hat and glasses on this day that I really love!

I also got this photo of Spaceship Earth on the Skyliner.

For our final day at Epcot and Disney World as a whole, we ate in Japan. While I don't have any photos of it I'd also love to shout out the Regal Eagle smokehouse at American Adventure; I ate there about 4 times and absolutely loved it!

That night, I took this one last photo of myself and the final result of my hair after a week and a half in Florida. We left for our flight at 3am the next morning. Overall I had a great time! Sadly, the heat got to me a bit in the last few days, though.
