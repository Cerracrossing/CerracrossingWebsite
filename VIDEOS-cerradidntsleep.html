Cerra Didn't Sleep

Recorded 22-23 July, 2021

Back in July 2021, I saw Bo Burnham's Inside for the first time. My then-new special interest in Burnham's work combined with the themes of the special lead to one of my most intense dissociative episodes of 2021, which I decided to work through by trying to film my own unscripted special-length video partially inspired by Inside in one night. Needless to say, the result was one of the worst things I'd ever created, failing to explain even my most basic of thoughts and feelings, and I didn't release it until January 2022. Note that I had to split it into 3 parts in accordance with Github Pages' file size limitations.
